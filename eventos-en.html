HAPPINESS WEEK

The Challenge of Happiness Week is an activity carried out within the frame of the Happiness Week, created by Universidad Tecmilenio and the Institute for Wellbeing and Happiness, to celebrate the International Day of Happiness created by the United Nations.

The challenge is completing positive actions, generating positive impacts on the largest number of people possible. The actions are based on the elements of our model, the Wellness and Happiness Ecosystem, so together as a community and a country we may share our happiness and celebrate what is positive.
