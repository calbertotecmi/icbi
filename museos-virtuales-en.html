Inicio Virtual Museums


Take a virtual tour of the positive museums here!


Besides the experience within the museum, there are virtual tours generated with the objective of getting people from all locations and countries together so they live the experience of positive emotions through virtual reality.

The tours are unique and have:
• Activations to identify and practice different positive emotions.
• Practical exercises which have demonstrated they increase individuals' well-being.
• Expert videos inviting to explore the museum through a positive approach.
