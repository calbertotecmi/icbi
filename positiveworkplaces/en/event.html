LOCAL EVENTS

It's time to focus on positive! Give yourself a moment to be happy and review the activities which will be held in partnership with the Festival Internacional Santa Lucia. Learn how you can feel good every moment of your day.

Activities

Break your routine! Explore positive emotions while enjoying workshops, guided tours and events, art history and science from September 20th to November 11th in the following museums.

Museo Marco

"Postcards in seen"

Participate in a guided tour of the Rafael Lozano Hemmer Unstable Presence exposition and share your experience in the emotions mailbox sending and receiving postcards of the museum's visitors.

Wednesday 10:00 a 6:00 pm.
Saturday and Sunday 10:00 a 3:00 pm.
From September 20th to November 10th
Address: Zuazua y Jardón S/N Col. Centro Monterrey, N.L C.P. 64000

Horno 3

"An amazing world”

Planet Earth exhibition at Horno 3 using the SOSR system with projections of the planet, as well as audiovisual material on 4 screens. Public participation through questions and answers with the presenter and leaving a written message in the auditorium.

Tuesday to Friday 5:00 pm.
Saturdays and Sundays 1:30, 4:00 and 6:00 pm.
Del 20 de septiembre al 10 de noviembre
Address: Av. Fundidora y Adolfo Prieto (dentro de Parque Fundidora) Col. Obrera Monterrey, N.L. C.P. 64010

Planetario Alfa

"Exploring senses and emotions"

We will take a sensory journey by the Aviary and gardens of the Planetario Alfa. Through our senses we will discover the emotions that flourish while in contact with nature. We will depict in a stone or a sheet of paper the positive emotion that the tour made us feel.

Fridays, Saturdays and Sundays : 3:00 PM, 5:00 PM and 6:00 PM
From September 20th to October 26th
Address: Av. Roberto Garza Sada No. 1000 Col. Carrizalejo, San Pedro Garza García, N.L. C.P. 66254

Obispado

"Obispado through time"

On the occasion of the Museo Regional of Nuevo Leon anniversary, people will know the different conditions of the place through time until today as a museum and in this way know the emotions that each of the images mean to them through the faces that illustrate the 10 positive emotions: Joy, Gratitude, Serenity, Hope, Pride, Inspiration, Wonder, Love, Fun, Interest. * A virtual tour will be performed.

Tuesday to Sunday 09:00 a 5:00 pm.
From September 20th to November 10th
Address: Fray Rafael Jose Verger S/n Col. Centro Monterrey, N.L. C.P. 64100

Papalote museo del niño de Monterrey

“Messages"

Visitors will write in sheets of paper, messages that express their wishes, commitments, emotions and feelings in favor of environmental conservation and with a Buddy support, place their messages in the wishing tree.

Friday to Sunday: 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM.
From September 20th to November 10th
Address: Av. Fundidora y Adolfo Prieto (inside the Fundidora Park) Col. Obrera Monterrey, N.L. C.P. 64010

Bernabé de las casas

“Live prehistory in Mina! ”

The challenge will be to take a tour and observe the various museum exhibits and select one that is of your liking. Once you have made your choice, you will try to analyze the piece from both perspectives, negative and positive. Subsequently, the visitor will ask a series of questions that can help him reflect on his emotions, as well as a drawing of the selected piece during his tour.

Tuesday to Sunday: 10:00 to 6:00 PM.
From September 20th to November 10th
Address: Miguel Hidalgo 909 Col. Centro Mina, N.L. C.P. 65100

Metropolitano de Monterrey

"Guided tour and a walking conference"
"Artistic activation workshop for children and adolescents"
“Live sculpture with public participation, with Master Hector Olivares"

Guided tour of the FEMALE TRACES Exhibition while simultaneously giving a lecture on the role of women in various contexts, as social, cultural, artistic, scientific and sports. Inspirational activity remembering the contributions of women in various fields such as Culture, Art, Science and Sports.

Artistic activation workshop for children and young people taking as reference the works exhibited in the Ludic Dreams Temporary Exhibition. Four Peruvian painting grandmasters, a circus-themed exhibition, poetry, fantasy, in which creativity and imagination will be stimulated in an artistic and festive atmosphere.

Live Sculpture Workshop with the participation of the audience with the sculptor Hector Olivares. A very festive activity where coexistence and a sense of cooperation will be fostered among participants while at the same time they are introduced to sculpture.

Activity 1: Sunday September 29th, 6:00 PM
Activity 2: Sunday, October 6th, 6:00 PM
Activity 3: Sunday October 13th, 6:00 PM
Address: Zaragoza s/n Col. Centro Monterrey, N.L. C.P. 64000

Museo del dulce

"Sweet Memories"

During the Museo del Dulce (Candy Museum) guided tour, visitors travel through time and space, reliving childhood memories in the old and traditional Monterrey, which can lead to emotions such as nostalgia, bringing forth happy moments of the past. During the tasting, we will reflect on positive emotions, of why sweets are socially associated with goodness and richness, and the feelings you experienced during the tour remembering your own anecdotes, to embody and share your experiences exchanging sweets with other visitors.

Monday to Friday: 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM.
Saturdays and Sundays: 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM.
From September 20th to November 10th
Address: Diego de Montemayor 444 (debajo del Museo de Historia Mexicana) Col. Centro Monterrey, N.L. C.P. 64000

Museo de cera - MUSCERA

“Strengths in wax”

Museo de Cera invites you to live "Positive emotions" in a tour in which you will ask and discover the strengths of character of the most emblematic figures that have left a mark in the world. In addition, you will work in a self-exploration to learn about the strengths that identify you and you will have fun sculpting them in modeling clay and exhibit them to visitors.

Saturdays and Sundays: 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM.
From September 20th to November 10th
Address: Av. Fundidora y Adolfo Prieto inside the Fundidora Park) Col. Obrera Monterrey, N.L. C.P. 64010

September, Sundays 22nd and 29th: 4:00 PM.
October, Sundays 6th, 13th and 20th: 4:00 PM.
November, Sundays 3rd and 10th: 4:00 PM
